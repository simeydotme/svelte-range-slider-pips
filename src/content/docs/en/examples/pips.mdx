Here we demonstrate the **Slider's main feature; "notches"**, or as I call them; **"`pips`"**. These little markers sit below 
the Slider by default to mark regular intervals along the Slider's length.

  <Pips client:visible />
  <Code slot="svelte" code={cleanImport(PipsRaw)} lang="svelte" />
</Demo>

<Note title="Pip Spread / Placement" type="info">
  The pips are placed according to [the `step` & `pipStep` algorithm](/svelte-range-slider-pips/en/examples/steps-combined).
</Note>




## Control the Display

Pip display is controlled by the properties; `all`, `first`, `last` and `rest`. 
These properties can be set to `true` or `false` to control the display of the pips.

<Info>This is also true for `labels`. [See more about `labels` here](/svelte-range-slider-pips/en/examples/pip-labels)</Info>

<Demo name="Controlling the Display" client:visible>
  <PipDisplay client:visible />
  <div slot="svelte">
    ```svelte
    <RangeSlider pips bind:all bind:first bind:last bind:rest />
    ```
  </div>
</Demo>
