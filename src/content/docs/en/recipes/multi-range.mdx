While it's not actually an `option` in the `<RangeSlider />` component, you can 
visually (and functionally) achieve multi-ranges by applying a gradient to the
`rangeBar` element, and setting the range values on the `style` prop.

Technically speaking, the below slider is not a `range` but simply a slider with
4 handles.

<Demo name="Multi-Range" panel="svelte" client:visible dark="auto">
  <MultiRange client:visible />
</Demo>

There's a couple of caveats to note for this approach, though;
1) Cannot use `draggy` as it's not a `range`, so the usability could be less than ideal.
2) The `rangeFloat` prop is also not going to work because it's not a `range`.
3) The `values` is a flat array, so you'll need to extract the values manually.
